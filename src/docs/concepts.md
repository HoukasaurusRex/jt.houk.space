# Performance and Impact Definitions

## "Performance" Defined

**The demonstration of results based on role expectations and operational context.**

| Level                       | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| --------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Not Performing**          | Delivers a moderate or inconsistent level of results, considering expectations for the role and the operational context. Might even be consistently below the expectations. Often misses agreed-upon goals.                                                    |
| **Performing**              | Delivers solid or exceptional results, considering expectations for the role and the operational context. Might even be exceeding expectations beyond core position's expectations. Achieves or occasionally exceeds agreed-upon goals.                        |
| **Outstanding Performance** | Delivers exceptional results, considering expectations for the role and the operational context. Exceeds expectations beyond what the role requires. Consistently exceeds agreed-upon goals and operates well beyond their current scope.                      |

## "Impact" Defined

**Impact reflects the employee's behavior at work. It looks at how they go about achieving their goals.**

| Level                  | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| ---------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Low Impact**         | Having a low or negative impact on the team or on Lightspeed overall. Exhibits patterns of behaviors that conflict with our Guiding Principles. Has difficulties learning from experience, or changing their behaviors. Shows little to no improvements, despite efforts from employee and leader.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| **Positive Impact**    | Exhibits behaviors that reflect our Guiding Principles, with no pattern of behaviors that conflict with them. Moderate to high level of learning agility - able to work within ambiguity. Shows potential for having increased impact within the team, department or Lightspeed overall. Adapts well to feedback.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| **Outstanding Impact** | Has significant impact in the company, leads by example, inspires major improvements in the outputs and outcomes of the team(s) they work with. Consistently exhibits behaviors that reflect our Guiding Principles visibly and in an exemplary manner. Provides and implements forward-thinking and creative solutions to their team, division, or Lightspeed. Demonstrates ability to rise to more senior roles and/or higher impact skills/competencies. Proactively takes steps to invest in their professional development. Seeks out assignments that provide variety, challenge and stretch beyond current expectations. A change agent who champions opportunities for greater outcomes/success for their team, function, or Lightspeed. Shares their know-how with team members and is seen as a 'go-to' in the team and/or the organization. |

|                        | **Not Performing** | **Performing** | **Outstanding Performance** |
