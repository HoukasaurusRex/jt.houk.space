## Investigations

Investigated a Merrco gift card issue. Merrco is one of the few payment processors that works with cannabis retailers, so when their integration breaks, there's no easy fallback.

## Team

Worked on the on-call rotation schedule. Distributing on-call fairly across the team matters for morale, especially when incidents tend to cluster.

## Incidents

A production fire alarm went off that required reverting a transactions PR. When a change to the transaction processing pipeline causes issues in production, the safest response is to roll back immediately and debug from a stable state rather than trying to fix forward under pressure.
