## Projects

Talked with Juliana from the localization team about how to provide good context with translation keys for the new Sales History filters we are adding. She shared a developer guide on localization best practices, which helped me improve several of our keys. Clear context in translation keys makes it easier for translators to understand where and how each string appears in the POS UI, reducing the chance of awkward or incorrect translations.
