## Team

### Architecture Meeting

Discussed the surcharge feature. Adding surcharges to cannabis transactions has regulatory implications since some jurisdictions restrict what fees
can be applied and how they're displayed on receipts.

### Post Mortem

Reviewed an incident where a web distribution was accidentally destroyed. Walked through the timeline and identified gaps in the deployment safeguards
that allowed it to happen.

## Projects

### Sales Channels

Discussed how parked sales should apply discounts at the time of payment rather than at the time the sale was created. This matters for dispensaries
that hold orders (for example, delivery or pre-orders) where pricing may change between when the order is placed and when it's fulfilled.

## Investigations

Looked into fixing a removed tax group for Flint & Embers. When a tax group gets deleted but existing products still reference it,
the POS can show incorrect pricing or fail to calculate taxes at checkout.
