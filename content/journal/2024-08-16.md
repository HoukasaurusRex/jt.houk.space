## Team

### Sprint Retro

Today during our retro we discussed ways to address fail points in our tickets. We decided to walk through our QA checklist in demo videos before moving tickets to "Ready for Test," and to document acceptance criteria from our epic breakdowns in a shared reference doc.

We also identified a need for more platform-wide collaboration. Going forward, we'll be more active in asking developers in #architecture for input on how our work impacts other teams. I plan to be more proactive in reaching out to other team leads before we start a new epic to consider the full scope.
