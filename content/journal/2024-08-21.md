## Team

### Leads Meeting

Discussed stability concerns and how to prioritize observability and maintenance tasks over feature development. The tension between shipping features
and keeping the platform reliable is a recurring theme, especially with the team absorbing more responsibility after the chief architect's departure.

### Architecture Meeting

Reviewed the scope of KML-based delivery zones. This feature will let dispensaries define geographic delivery boundaries using KML files,
which the system needs to validate against customer addresses during checkout.

## Investigations

WooCommerce integration was failing for Prairie Cannabis. Their IT team suspected an IP filter issue, but I found authorization errors in the logs
pointing to a token problem instead. Also ran inventory submissions for Ganjika House covering July's data to get their OCS compliance reports caught
up.
