## Investigations

Investigated how cannabinoid data flows from sold lots back to the ecom service. When a lot is sold, the cannabinoid percentages (THC, CBD)
need to stay accurate in the online store so customers see correct product information.

## Projects

Worked on a register endpoint and a partial compliance report for Manitoba. Each province has its own reporting format, and Manitoba's requirements
differ enough from Ontario's that it needs its own report template.

Fixed a bug where auto discounts were failing after midnight UTC. The discount eligibility check was using UTC timestamps,
which meant discounts scheduled to end at midnight local time were actually cutting off hours early or late depending on the retailer's time zone.
